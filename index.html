Visit Minneapolis

An incredibly rich and diverse area with dramatic skylines, and natural beauty of dozens of trails, parks and waterways.

Top Three Activities To do At Minneapolis:

Nature & Outdoors

City by Nature…literally. In Minneapolis, you’re never farther than 6 blocks away from a park. Our vast urban wilderness is waiting for you.

Family Fun

Spend all day cruising and splashing at the Mall of America or playing mini golf the Sculpture Garden. Pack a picnic and head to Minnehaha Falls.

Arts & Culture

Breathtaking murals, world-class museums, inspiring art fairs, incredible Broadway shows, and one giant spoon.
